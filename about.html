Who I am

About Me

Hello, I’m Jerrad — a software engineer with a background in data analysis and a deep love for building reliable, human-friendly web apps. My work blends structured logic with creative design to solve practical problems.

Before moving into software engineering, I worked in data analytics and business intelligence, focusing on dashboards, automation, and process improvement. I’m passionate about creating tools that make people’s lives easier — whether it’s through data clarity or better user interfaces.

Interests

  • Front-end development (React, TypeScript, CSS)
  • API and database design (Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL)
  • Outdoor recreation — hiking, cycling, and rock climbing
  • Cooking and culinary technique (Rouxbe-trained home cook)

Philosophy

I believe good software should be clean, accessible, and intentional — built with empathy for both users and developers. My approach values performance, maintainability, and simplicity over trend chasing.

Currently based in Utah, I’m planning an international move to Peru to continue working remotely and explore a more minimalist, meaningful lifestyle.
