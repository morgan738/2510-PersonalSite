Favorite Foods

If I had to eat the same 3 foods for the rest of my life these would be my Top 3

  • #1 Bandeja Paisa

    A Bandeja Paisa is one of, if not thee dish that Colombia is known for. This is my #1 because as you can see it has a little bit of evrything. Rice, beans, chicharon, steak, egg, avocado, maduro, arepa, and sausage. You're definitely not having any room for dessert after this!

  • #2 Encebollado

    The Encebollado is one of Ecuador's most popular dishes and it is essentially a fish soup. I love this soup and it is a close consideration for #1 spot!

  • #3 Pepperoni Pizza

    Who doesn't love a good pepperoni pizza? You could basically pay me in pizza. If you ever need help moving your furniture and say you're getting us pizza, I'm there!
